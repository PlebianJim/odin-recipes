Mock Goat Curry

Description

Curry goat is a Caribbean staple - now you can enjoy this intensely delicious and flavoursome dish - vegan style. This recipe is guaranteed to hit the spot.

Ingredients

    Vegan Goat Meat

  • 1.5 cup vital wheat gluten
  • 1.5 cup cooked brown lentils
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp corriandor powder
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • nutmeg
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 0.5 cup water

    • Curry

  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp pimento
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 scotch bonnets
  • 2 cups vegetable stock

Method

  1. Roughly mash cooked lentils in a large bowl. Add vital wheat gluten flour, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, chili powder, nutmeg powder, salt, black pepper, onion powder, coriander powder, all-purpose seasoning, and water. Knead together well for approx. 10-15 minutes (the better you knead your mixture, the better your results). Cut seitan pieces into 1-inch meaty slabs and set aside.
  2. Fry onions and garlic in a large saucepan on medium to high heat until they become soft, approx. 2 minutes. Add curry powder and seitan pieces. Fry seitan pieces for a couple of minutes whilst stirring to combine with curry powder.
  3. Add sweet peppers and sweet potatoes and continue to fry, stirring occasionally. Add more oil if required.
  4. Add thyme, bay leaf, spring onion, scotch bonnet, and 2 cups of vegetable broth.
  5. Cook for 35 minutes on a low to medium heat whilst mix thickens. Add a little more water if necessary, do not let the mixture dry out. Add black pepper and salt to taste.
  6. Serve with accompaniments of your choice. Pairs well with rice and peas.
